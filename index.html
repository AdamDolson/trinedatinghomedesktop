Charts

Whether you're learning about your unique self or getting a clearer picture of a relationship, you can use our chart generators to create engaging and informative blueprints of the sky.

Synastry is all about the comparison between energies, observing how individual personalities interact, and determining complementary or detrimental effects.

Your natal chart is a map of the zodiac at the moment you were born, a guide helping you understand the synchronistic signatures of your own personality and potential impact.

Composite is all about interpreting the merging of energies, deriving harmonious or disharmonious middlegrounds between the charts of two individuals.
